About Benteng Vredeburg

Museum Benteng Vredeburg is a former Dutch fortress located in Yogyakarta, Indonesia. Built in 1765, it has witnessed significant historical events and now serves as a museum showcasing the struggle for Indonesian independence.

The museum offers visitors a unique glimpse into the colonial era and the fight for freedom through its extensive collection of artifacts, dioramas, and historical documents.

Today, Benteng Vredeburg stands as a symbol of resilience and a testament to Indonesia's rich cultural heritage.
